How To

Rabbits Daily Care

Litter Box

Supplies

  • Timothy Hay or Alfalfa Hay
  • Hooded Cat Litter Box
  • Cedar Shaving or News Paper
  • Optional: Large Trash Bag

Steps

  1. Grab Hooded Cat Litter Box. Remove top.
  2. Optional: Put Large Trash Bag at the bottom of the litter box
  3. Put Cedar Shavings or News Paper at the bottom of the litter box (after trash bag)
  4. Put large amounts of hay at the entrance of the litter box
  5. Put lid back on, and give to your rabbits. (change litter box 2-3 times a week)

Daily Food

Supplies

  • Timothy Rabbit Pellets or Alfalfa Rabbit Pellets
  • Hay
  • Spring Mix
  • Snacks: Carrots, Bananas, Fruits, and store bought snacks

Steps

  1. Morning: Depending on how old your rabbit is give them that many pellets
  2. Morning: Throw in a hand full of spring mix
  3. Evening: This is prime snack time. Only give them one to no snacks a day
  4. Night: Follow the same steps as the morning steps
  5. ALWAYS make sure they have water avalible

Grooming

Supplies

  • Pet Brush (I would suggest Hair Buster)
  • Cat nail clippers
  • Nail file

Steps

  1. Look up videos on how to brush your rabbits. (yt channel I recommend is Lennon the Bunny)
  2. Check on their nails every now and then
  3. If their nails need cliped, then clip them.
  4. Follow steps 2 and 3 for filing too.

Entertainment

Supplies

  • Toilet Paper Rolls
  • Pine Cones
  • Leaves
  • Store Bought Toys
  • MOST IMPORTANT: you.
  • Optional: another rabbit

Steps

  1. Give them toys when bored
  2. Look up DIY rabbit toys (Channels: Lennon the Bunny and checker_bunnies)
  3. You could get another rabbit so your rabbit has someone to play with
  4. Make sure YOU play with them.
